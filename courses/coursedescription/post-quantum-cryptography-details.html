What You Will Master

  • Analyze the threats of Shor's and Grover's algorithms to modern cryptography.
  • Understand the NIST PQC standardization process and the selected algorithms.
  • Master the principles of lattice-based cryptography, including LWE and Module-LWE.
  • Implement the CRYSTALS-Kyber KEM and CRYSTALS-Dilithium signature schemes.
  • Explore the foundations of code-based, hash-based, and multivariate cryptography.
  • Design hybrid systems and protocols (like TLS) for a crypto-agile migration.
  • Analyze implementation security, side-channel attacks, and performance optimization.
  • Develop a strategic framework for an organizational transition to PQC.
