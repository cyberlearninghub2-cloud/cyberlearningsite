SSL/TLS Basics

A comprehensive, 20-lesson course on the principles of secure communication, digital certificates, and the TLS protocol.

100% Free Course

What You Will Master

  • Understand the core differences between HTTP and the security guarantees of HTTPS.
  • Deconstruct the TLS Handshake process, from the ClientHello to the final Finished message.
  • Explain the role of Certificate Authorities (CAs) and the Chain of Trust.
  • Differentiate between public/private keys and the symmetric keys used in a TLS session.
  • Compare the security features of modern protocols like TLS 1.2 and TLS 1.3.
  • Learn to identify and mitigate common TLS attacks like POODLE and Heartbleed.
  • Configure a web server (Apache/Nginx) for secure TLS deployment.
  • Understand advanced concepts like Mutual TLS (mTLS) and Certificate Revocation (CRL/OCSP).
