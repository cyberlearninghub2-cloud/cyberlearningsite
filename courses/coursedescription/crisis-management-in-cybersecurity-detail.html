Master the art and science of managing cybersecurity crises, from initial incident response to strategic communication, legal considerations, and executive leadership.

What You Will Master

  • Differentiate between a security 'incident' and a full-blown 'crisis'.
  • Apply the NIST Incident Response framework to manage a crisis lifecycle.
  • Develop and execute a strategic crisis communication plan for all stakeholders.

  • Navigate complex legal and regulatory requirements, including GDPR and cyber insurance.
  • Lead a Crisis Management Team (CMT) and make effective decisions under pressure.
  • Design and facilitate realistic tabletop exercises to test and improve your plans.
  • Use metrics like MTTD and MTTR to evaluate and mature your crisis management program.
