Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)

A comprehensive course on how trust, keys, and certificates are managed at scale to secure the internet, from enterprise networks to the post-quantum future.

100% Free Course

What You Will Master

  • Design and understand PKI trust models, including hierarchical CAs and the Web of Trust.
  • Analyze and decode X.509 digital certificates to validate identity and trust.
  • Implement secure private key management using hardware security modules (HSMs) and best practices.
  • Manage the complete certificate lifecycle, including issuance, renewal, and revocation (CRL/OCSP).
  • Deploy and manage an enterprise PKI using Active Directory Certificate Services (AD CS).
  • Secure email with S/MIME, and ensure software integrity with code signing certificates.
  • Implement certificate-based authentication for VPNs, smart cards, and IoT devices.
  • Understand the threat of quantum computing to PKI and the transition to post-quantum cryptography.
