Cloud Compliance Frameworks

A practical guide to designing, implementing, and managing compliance programs for major frameworks like SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS, and HIPAA in the cloud.

100% Free Course

What You Will Master

  • Master the requirements of major frameworks: SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS, and HIPAA.
  • Understand the Cloud Shared Responsibility Model for IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS.
  • Implement a compliant Information Security Management System (ISMS) based on ISO 27001.
  • Design and enforce least-privilege access using IAM, PAM, and Zero Trust principles.
  • Prepare for and manage third-party audits and learn to automate evidence collection.
  • Navigate international data privacy laws like GDPR and cross-border data transfer rules.
  • Automate compliance with DevSecOps, including Infrastructure as Code (IaC) and Policy as Code (PaC).
  • Develop a complete compliance program, including governance, budgeting, and reporting.
