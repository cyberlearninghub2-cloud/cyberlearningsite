Digital Forensics Introduction

A comprehensive course on the theory and practice of digital forensics, covering evidence handling, file system analysis, and investigation of modern platforms like cloud and mobile.

100% Free Course

What You Will Master

  • Understand the legal and ethical principles of forensic investigation.
  • Perform forensic analysis on Windows (NTFS), Linux (EXT4), and macOS (APFS) file systems.
  • Conduct memory forensics to find evidence of fileless malware and runtime activity.
  • Apply forensic techniques to cloud, mobile, IoT, and vehicle investigations.
  • Use timeline analysis to correlate evidence and reconstruct complex attack sequences.
  • Write court-ready forensic reports and prepare for expert witness testimony.
  • Master forensic imaging, evidence handling, and chain of custody procedures.
  • Use industry-standard tools like Autopsy, Volatility, and Wireshark.
