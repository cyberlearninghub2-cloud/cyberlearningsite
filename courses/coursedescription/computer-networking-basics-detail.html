Computer Networking Basics

Understand how computer networks work, from core protocols and devices to practical troubleshooting and security fundamentals.

100% Free Course

What You Will Master

  • Learn the 7 layers of the OSI model and the 4 layers of the TCP/IP model.
  • Differentiate between essential devices like routers, switches, hubs, and firewalls.
  • Master IPv4 addressing, understand subnetting, and learn the structure of IPv6.
  • Compare connection-oriented (TCP) and connectionless (UDP) protocols and their use cases.
  • Understand the critical roles of DNS and DHCP in network operations.
  • Explore Wi-Fi standards and the security protocols (WPA2/WPA3) used to protect wireless networks.
  • Use command-line tools like `ping`, `traceroute`, and `nslookup` for basic troubleshooting.
  • Design a complete small office network, from the physical topology to the IP addressing scheme.
