Cryptography Essentials

Learn the foundations of cryptography, from classical ciphers to modern algorithms and their practical applications in cybersecurity.

100% Free Course

What You Will Master

  • Understand symmetric encryption and the modern AES standard.
  • Grasp asymmetric cryptography, including RSA and Elliptic Curve Cryptography (ECC).
  • Learn how hash functions (SHA-256) and Message Authentication Codes (HMAC) provide data integrity.
  • Implement digital signatures to ensure authenticity and non-repudiation.
  • Understand the Diffie-Hellman key exchange to establish secure communication channels.
  • Learn how Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) and digital certificates secure the web (HTTPS).
  • Apply secure password hashing techniques using bcrypt and Argon2.
  • Explore the future with an introduction to Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC).
